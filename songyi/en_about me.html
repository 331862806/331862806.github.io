I'm Jason Zou
A freelance photographer
Born in Shanghai in 1988. Living in Guangzhou at present. Capricorns.

Specializes in portrait photography; good at recording innocent and pure eyes and the upcoming passage of youth.

Many people never thought about recording their youth until it passes away at their 20s. With the flowing time, we can never get back to those moments, which can never measured by a price. One day you will get everything you want but not your youth. I hope I can record and freeze the moments when you are in your most beautiful state and in your best mood.
This is how I would go with a lifetime keeping the moments of your best days and looking for your best appearances.
